    Description:
    dsrm, Double-stranded RNA binding motif. 
      Putative motif shared by proteins that bind to dsRNA. At least some DSRM 
      proteins seem to bind to specific RNA targets. Exemplified by Staufen, 
      which is involved in localization of at least five different mRNAs in the 
      early Drosophila embryo. Also by interferon-induced protein kinase in 
      humans, which is part of the cellular response to dsRNA.
